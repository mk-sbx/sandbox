Evidence catalogue


Evidence Catalogue is a centralised repository of external databasets available internally and in the wild. It is meant as a central research tool and  

ECAT entries verification centered around two axes - what is (actually) available and how difficult is it to get (and convert). Some follow ups included:
- Does this link exist?
- Is the data there?
- How fresh is the data?
- Is the data actionable for answering the question?
- Is the data comprehensive and does it cover the area it claims to cover?
- Does the licence match the ECAT record?
- Are there alternatives to getting the particular dataset?
- Is there geographical location included?
- Is the geographical information expressed in a machine-native format?
